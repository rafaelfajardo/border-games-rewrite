To play La Migra use the d-pad to move the cursor over the blinking "Go To Work" icon and press the START button on gamepad.
Use the Y- and A-buttons to patrol the border. Use the X-button to fling handcuffs at the alien invaders. Catch as many as you can to be named agent of the month.
