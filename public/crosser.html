To play Crosser use the d-pad to move the cursor over the blinking start icon and press the START on gamepad.
Use the A, B, X and Y buttons to move Carlos across the border into the US. Avoid getting hit by the floating debris, you will have to start over. Avoid getting caught by la migra, you will be sent back. Sometimes la migra won't look, be quick. Get to the green card on the other side to pursue the American dream.
